Side Quest Shop Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Through our Pinterest integration and Etsy shop, we may collect:

  • Basic account information (name, email) when you connect with Pinterest
  • Order information (name, address, items purchased) through Etsy
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type) for Pinterest analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Process and fulfill Etsy orders
  • Show relevant Pinterest pins and boards
  • Improve our product offerings
  • Respond to customer inquiries

3. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Pinterest for product inspiration and sharing
  • Etsy for order processing and payments

These services have their own privacy policies which we recommend reviewing.

4. Data Security

We rely on Etsy and Pinterest's security measures to protect your data. We don't store payment information directly.

5. Your Choices

You can:

  • Disconnect Pinterest integration at any time
  • Manage your Etsy account settings
  • Contact us to request data deletion

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. The latest version will always be available here.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through our Etsy shop.
